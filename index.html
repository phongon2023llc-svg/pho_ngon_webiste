Our Story

Authentic Vietnamese Food Near Plymouth Meeting

At Pho Ngon, we bring the rich culinary traditions of Vietnam to Montgomery County, Pennsylvania. Located on Germantown Pike just 5 minutes from Plymouth Meeting Mall and Our Town Alley, our restaurant serves authentic Vietnamese cuisine with recipes crafted with care, using fresh ingredients and time-honored techniques.

From our signature pho noodle soup — simmered for hours to achieve the perfect depth of flavor — to our crisp, savory bánh mì Vietnamese sandwiches and fragrant bún rice vermicelli bowls, every dish tells a story of heritage and passion. We're proud to be the go-to Vietnamese restaurant for East Norriton, Plymouth Meeting, Germantown, King of Prussia, and surrounding areas.

Whether you're dining in, getting takeout, or ordering delivery, we invite you to experience the warmth of Vietnamese hospitality with every visit to our Germantown Pike location. Conveniently located near Plymouth Meeting Mall, we're your neighborhood Vietnamese restaurant.
